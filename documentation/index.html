Algoliasearch Helper

The toolbox for creating instant-search experiences

Build instant-search experiences your way!

Algolia Helper provides rock solid foundations for making your own instantsearch UX with your tools.

Used by instantsearch.js, its architecture lets you create advanced search with the speed of Algolia.

Get Started

The right architecture for the right search experience

The helper proposes a way to structure your search, so you can spent time on your users search

  • Parameters tracking - the helper is the search source of truth
  • Event based - read and write operations can be decoupled
  • High level features - easy access to advanced facetting techniques and more
  • And more - smart behaviours, chainability...
Learn more about the concepts

The helper is any-framework friendly

The Algoliasearch Helper is first and foremost an agnostic library. It plays well with the big players in town, and we will always make sure that it is usable with your champion.
